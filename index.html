About Me

Hi, my name is Louise Golding and besides being a huge animal lover, I have over 10 years experience dog walking and boarding

I am CRB checked and, as a veterinary nurse, can administer medication and also have animal first aid skills.

I transport dogs in a specially adapted van, and follow all safety procedures during walks including GPS trackers. Your dog will only be off lead if you specifically request it and if I'm satisfied that it is safe to do so.

All dogs boarding will stay at our home which I share with my partner Joe and our very sociable rescue pup Sherlock. Your dogs will receive the best care and attention, and I will treat them as if they were my own

I'm based in Coulsdon, and cover surrounding areas up to 10 miles

Please feel free to drop me a message through the booking section and we can discuss any specific requests or concerns that you have! I'm very flexible and will always try to find a way to fit you in if possible, even at short notice
