Visits


Do you need to leave your pet at home for long periods? We can pop in for a 30 minute visit(s) to provide company and cuddles, top up their water and make sure they're well. We can administer medication if required and we also offer this service for cats and other small animals. This is ideal for young or older pets who need more attention or are not able to go for walks.

Walks


Don't worry about your pooch when you go to work: they'll have the best adventures with us! We'll pick them up to go for a fun-filled walk. Walks last 30/60 minutes and we provide individual and group walks, with a maximum of 4 dogs per group. Once back home we'll towel them down, refill their water and make sure they're happy and ready to welcome you back after their nap.

Boarding


Going on holiday but worry about leaving your pet behind? With our boarding service, your dog will have their own holiday and the best time! We'll take care of them in our home, giving them all the attention that they're used to. They'll have two walks a day, play and cuddles and will have a great time whilst you're away, safe in the knowledge that they're well cared for.
