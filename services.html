Paw-Some Services

This is our offer for your pets

  • Home visits


    Do you need to leave your pet at home for long periods when you go to work or out for fun? We can help. We will pop in for a 30 minute visit to play with your dog, give them some company and cuddles, top up their water and make sure they are well and happy. We can also administer medication if required. We mostly take care of dogs but we are also able to offer this service for cats and other small animals such as rabbits, hamsters, etc. This service is ideal for young pups or older pets who are not very energetic and may need attention more often. We can visit twice a day or more often if needed.

  • Dog walking


    Don't worry about your pooch when you spend long hours at work anymore: they will be having the best walks and adventures with us! We will pick them up at your desired time in a dog-safe air-conditioned van, and take them out for a long, fun-filled walk. Every walk lasts one hour from the moment that we get to the park and until we get back into the van. We can provide individual walks or group walks, with a maximum of 4 dogs per group. Once back home, we will towel your pooch down, fill their water bowl and make sure they are all relaxed and ready to welcome you back at the end of the day.

  • Dog Boarding


    Would you like to go on holiday but feel guilty about leaving your four-legged friend behind? Feel guilty no more. With our boarding service, your dog will have a holiday of their own and the best time! We will take care of them in our home, giving them all the care and attention that they are used to at home and in a family setting. They will have lovely walks, lots of play and cuddles, treats galore and will generally just have a great time whilst you relax safe in the knowledge that your best friend is well cared for and having a great time. They will be exercised and nourished, and we can even throw in some training to keep them sharp if you so wish.
