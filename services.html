Paw-Some Pet Services

Visits

Do you need to leave your pet at home for long periods when you go to work or out for fun? We can help. We'll pop in for a 30 minute visit to play with your dog, give them some company and cuddles, top up their water and make sure that they're well. We can also administer oral medication if required. We also offer this service for cats and other small animals such as rabbits, hamsters, etc. This service is ideal for young pups or older pets who may need more personalised attention or are not able to go for walks. We can visit twice a day or more often if needed.

Price

£10 per each 30 minute visit

Walks

Don't worry about your pooch when you spend long hours at work: they'll have the best adventures with us! We'll pick them up and take them out for a fun-filled walk. Walks last 30 or 60 minutes from the moment we get to the park to when we get back to the van. We provide individual and group walks, with a maximum of 4 dogs per group. Once back home we'll towel them down, refill their water and make sure they're happy and ready to welcome you back after their nap.

Price

30 min group walk: £10 / 60 min group walk: £13

30 min solo walk: £14 / 60 min solo walk: £16

*50% discount for second pet in household

Boarding

Would you like to go on holiday but worry about leaving your pet behind? With our home boarding service, your dog will have a holiday of their own and the best time! We'll take care of them in our home, giving them all attention that they're used to. They'll get two lovely walks per day, lots of play and cuddles, treats galore and will just have a great time whilst you are away, safe in the knowledge that your best friend is well cared for. We can even throw in some training to keep them sharp if you so wish.

Price

£30 for each 24 hour stay per dog

*50% discount for second pet in household
